Grade percentage: (Points Earned √∑ Total Points) √ó 100. Common grade scales: 90-100% = A, 80-89% = B, 70-79% = C, 60-69% = D, below 60% = F. Grade percentages help track academic progress and maintain educational standards.

Markup percentage: ((Selling Price - Cost Price) √∑ Cost Price) √ó 100. Selling price = Cost Price √ó (1 + Markup% √∑ 100). Markup calculations are essential for retail pricing, profit margin analysis, and competitive positioning.
